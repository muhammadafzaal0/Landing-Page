FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FocusFuel Gummies are a delectable and convenient way to fuel your day. Carefully crafted to provide a balanced energy boost, these gummies are perfect for those who prefer a flavorful alternative to traditional caffeinated beverages..

Each FocusFuel Gummy contains 50 milligrams of natural caffeine and each serving (2 Gummies) contains 100 milligrams of natural caffeine, offering a precisely measured dose to keep you alert without the need for coffee or energy drinks.

Pregnant or nursing women should exercise caution with caffeine consumption. High levels of caffeine during pregnancy may be associated with an increased risk of miscarriage or preterm birth. It's advisable for expectant mothers to consult with their healthcare provider to determine a safe caffeine intake level.

Individuals with ADHD may experience varying responses to caffeine. While some may find it helps with focus and attention, others may be more sensitive to its stimulant effects. It's recommended for individuals with ADHD to monitor their caffeine intake and observe how it affects their symptoms. Consultation with a healthcare professional can provide personalized guidance.
